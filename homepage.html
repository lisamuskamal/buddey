About Us

Malaysia has abundance of musical and performing arts talents, yet finding suitable venues for performances can often be a cumbersome and challenging process. Having understand the challenges faced by the local talents, the founders of Buddey launched Buddey Talent app in 2020 as a solution to the local talents.
The platform was designed to bridge the gap for talents to easily find performing arts space. The platform also simplifies talent search for job opportunities, thus enabling arts and culture talents to generate potential side income. By connecting talents with venues and opportunities, Buddey Talent app aims fosters a vibrant arts community and supports the growth of Malaysia's cultural landscape.

Partner With Us

Become our Venue Partner to create a Buskers Pod at your space for local talents to engaged in street performances. This adds a creative and cultural dimension to your venue. There are no charges to the Venue Partner for the creation of the Buskers Pod.
The Buskers Pod is a fully hands-free system for Venue Partners, requiring little to no effort in managing the activities. Talents select and set their own activity plans within the parameters of the Buskers Pod. All talents are required to pass e-KYC due diligence to ensure safety and fair use of the site. Join our network of over 50 venues in creating Buskers Pods for the local talents.

Talent

The Buddey Talent App is designed to empower the performing arts community. With features such as Buskers Pod for street performances, talents can easily find, pick and choose the place, date and time to book the performing arts spaces. The Buddey Talent Jobs section helps talents to find job opportunities specifically in the performing arts sector. Additionally, the marketplace at Bucketlis.com offers a platform for talents to buy and sell secondhand musical instruments.
Buddey Talent App service is available on the Play Store, App Store, Huawei App, and web browser, the Buddey Talent App aims to promote, encourage, and nurture performing arts talents across Malaysia.
